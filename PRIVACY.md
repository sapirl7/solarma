# Privacy Policy

## Data Collection

### What We Collect
- **Alarm settings**: Time, wake proof configuration, deposit preferences
- **Wake proof completion**: Success/failure status (not raw sensor data)
- **Wallet address**: Your public Solana address (for transactions)

### What We DO NOT Collect
- Raw sensor data (steps, accelerometer readings)
- NFC tag identifiers (we store hashes only)
- Location data
- Personal identification information

## Data Storage

### On-Device
- Alarm history stored locally (Room database)
- Preferences stored locally (DataStore)
- NFC/QR hashes stored locally and on-chain

### On-Chain
- Alarm records (public by nature of blockchain)
- Deposit amounts and penalty routes
- Timestamps and status

## Third Parties

We do not share data with third parties except:
- **Solana blockchain**: Transaction data is public
- **RPC providers**: Necessary for blockchain interaction

## Data Deletion

- Delete app to remove local data
- On-chain data cannot be deleted (blockchain immutability)
